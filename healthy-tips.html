4 Ways to Eat Healthy

1

Choose Nutritious Foods

Eat foods rich in nutrients, such as fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean or low-fat proteins.

2

Portion Size

It is important to pay attention to food portion sizes. Try to ensure that the portions you consume are balanced and in accordance with your body's energy needs.

3

Eat a Variety of Foods

Make sure to eat a variety of foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, plant and animal proteins.

4

Limit Fast Food

Limit consumption of processed foods and fast foods that are high in sugar, saturated fat and salt. These foods tend to be low in nutrients and high in calories.
